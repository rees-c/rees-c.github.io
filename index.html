    Highlights

    • May 2025 We released a pre-print, "Space Group Equivariant Crystal Diffusion", detailing SGEquiDiff, our crystal generative model which generates crystals with space group invariant likelihoods. SGEquiDiff yields SOTA performance on novelty and stability metrics as assessed by density functional theory calculations.
      [Paper]
    • March 2025 We developed an autoregressive crystal generative model, WyckoffAUGen, with SOTA sampling speeds and explicit, space group invariant conditional likelihoods. Details are in our paper, "Crystal Generative Modeling with Explicit Autoregressive Conditional Likelihoods and Nontrivial Space Group Stabilizers", accepted to ICLR AI4Mat 2025.
      [Paper]
    • November 2022 Our paper, "Towards overcoming data scarcity in materials science: unifying models and datasets with a mixture of experts framework", is published in npj Computational Materials. We leveraged mixtures of experts to do scalable multi-source transfer learning for materials regression tasks, yielding SOTA performance and interpretable transferability scores between pre-training and downstream tasks.
      [Paper]
